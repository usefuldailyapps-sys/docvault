Privacy Policy

DocuSafe — Personal Document Organizer

Effective: March 21, 2026

DocuSafe ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Profile names and emojis — names you give to profiles (e.g., "Dad", "Mom")
  • Document data — document names, notes, and categories you create
  • Photos and files — images you capture or import as document pages
  • Profile pictures — photos you set as profile avatars

Automatically Collected

  • OCR text — text extracted from scanned images for search functionality (processed on-device)
  • Google account info — name, email, and profile photo when you sign in for backup

Important: All your documents and data are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your files, and they are never uploaded to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To organize and display your documents within the app
  • To enable full-text search across your documents via OCR
  • To back up your data to your own Google Drive (only when you choose to)
  • To restore your data from your Google Drive backup
  • To show relevant advertisements (via Google AdMob)

3. Google Drive Backup

When you enable cloud backup:

  • Your database and document files are uploaded to your personal Google Drive
  • Files are stored in a "DocuSafe Backups" folder visible in your Drive
  • We use the drive.file scope — we can only access files created by DocuSafe
  • We cannot access your other Google Drive files
  • You can delete the backup at any time from your Google Drive

4. Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

Google Sign-In

We use Google Sign-In solely for authentication to enable Google Drive backup. We only access your basic profile information (name, email, photo).

ML Kit (OCR)

Text recognition is performed entirely on-device using Google ML Kit. No images are sent to any server for OCR processing.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device using SQLite
  • Biometric authentication (fingerprint/face) can be enabled to lock the app
  • Google Drive backups use your Google account's security
  • We do not operate any servers that store your data

6. In-App Purchases

We offer ad-removal subscriptions and one-time purchases. Payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not collect or store any payment information.

7. Children's Privacy

DocuSafe is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

8. Your Rights

  • Delete your data: Uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Delete backups: Remove the "DocuSafe Backups" folder from your Google Drive
  • Revoke access: Remove DocuSafe from your Google account's connected apps
  • Opt out of ads: Purchase an ad-free plan or adjust device ad settings

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective" date.
